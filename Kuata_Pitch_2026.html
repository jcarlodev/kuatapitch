DATA SOVEREIGNTY · OUR COMMITMENT
No equity stake for the state.
Full sovereignty guarantee.
Kuata remains 100% founder-led and venture-fundable. Data sovereignty is guaranteed through architecture and certification — not through ownership. Citizens' data never leaves Angola.

✓

✓

SOC 2 Type II — Security & Availability

Local Data Residency
All citizen data is stored in the AWS region closest to Angola — af-south-1 (Cape Town). Ministerial data processed on sovereign cloud infrastructure. No cross-border data transfer without consent.
Zero-Knowledge Architecture
Documents are cryptographically signed and verified without raw data exposure. Kuata never sees plaintext citizen data — only verified attestations.
Immutable Audit Trail
Every access event logged under Angolan Law 22/11. Citizens can see who accessed their data, when, and why. Full transparency by design.
